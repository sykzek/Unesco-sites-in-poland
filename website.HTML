Discover Poland's cultural and natural treasures

Historic Centre of Kraków

The historic centre of Kraków is one of the most important cultural heritage sites in Europe, featuring the Main Market Square, Wawel Castle, and medieval architecture.

Wieliczka Salt Mine

The Wieliczka Salt Mine, operating since the 13th century, is famous for its underground chapels, sculptures, and saline lakes carved entirely from salt.

Auschwitz-Birkenau

Auschwitz-Birkenau is a memorial and museum preserving the site of the former Nazi concentration and extermination camp, a powerful reminder of history.

Białowieża Forest

Białowieża Forest is one of the last and largest remaining parts of the primeval forest that once covered much of Europe, home to the European bison.
